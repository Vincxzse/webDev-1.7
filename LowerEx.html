Workout Tips and Plan

LOWER BODY WORKOUTS

MEN EXERCISE

  1. Romanian Deadlift

    Stand with feet hip-width apart, knees slightly bent. Hold a dumbbell in each hand at your thighs. Hinge at your hips and push your butt back, keeping your back flat. Your torso should be almost parallel to the floor, and the weights should reach your shins. Push through your heels to stand up straight. Pause at the top and squeeze your glutes.

  2. Goblet Squat

    Stand with feet wider than hip-width apart, holding a dumbbell in front of you. Engage your core and squat down by shifting your weight into your heels. Drive through your heels to stand and squeeze your glutes at the top.

  3. Glute Bridge

    Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor, with a dumbbell resting on your hips. Push through your heels to lift your hips while squeezing your glutes. Pause, then lower back down.

  4. Reverse Lunge

    Stand with feet shoulder-width apart. Step back with your right foot, landing on the ball of your foot, and lower into a lunge. Push through the heel of your left foot to return to starting position.

  5. Lateral Monster Walk

    Stand with feet hip-width apart and knees bent. Step to the right with your right foot, then bring the left in, but not all the way. Keep your hips down in a quarter-squat position.

WOMEN EXERCISE

  1. Hip Circles

    Support yourself on elbows and knees. Make circles in the air with one knee, moving from the hip. Continue for 30 seconds, then switch legs.

  2. Walking Lunges

    Stand with hands on hips. Step forward with right leg and lower into a lunge. Press through the right foot and step forward again with the left leg.

  3. Side Lying Plank

    Lie on your side, prop up on your elbow, and lift your hips off the ground, keeping your body in a straight line.

  4. Broad Jump

    Stand with feet hip-width apart. Bend at the hips and launch your body forward in a controlled jump. Land on your feet.

  5. Banded Lateral Walk

    Place a mini resistance band above your ankles. Stand with feet hip-width apart and step left, followed by right.

Aim for 8–12 reps of each exercise, resting 30 seconds between exercises and 1–2 minutes after each round. Complete 2–5 rounds total. This workout is suitable for those who want to strengthen their lower body.

Benefits: Lower body workouts help with weight control, digestion, gut health, and hormone regulation. They improve muscle and bone health, brain function, and cardiovascular fitness.
