Tips to Get Strong and Healthy Nails

Well-groomed nails enhance your appearance and can make hands look youthful.

  1. Keep your hands clean

    Wash hands thoroughly, removing polish with acetone-free remover. Use a toothbrush and soap to scrub nails and surrounding skin gently.

  2. Be gentle on your nails

    Avoid metal tools under nails to prevent separation. Use gloves for cleaning or washing dishes.

  3. Clip your nails regularly

    Trim nails every two weeks to reduce snags and breakage.

  4. Prioritize nail health over length

    Keep nails short to avoid breakage, especially if you struggle with hangnails.

  5. Always keep a nail file on hand

    Keep a nail file handy for on-the-spot buffing of rough edges, working in one direction.

  6. Wear gloves when cleaning

    Household cleaners can weaken nails. Wear gloves for protection.

  7. Moisturize your hands

    Apply lotion daily, especially after washing hands.

  8. Take a biotin supplement

    Biotin improves nail strength and health. Consult a healthcare professional before starting any supplement.

  9. Consider a manicure

    Regular manicures keep nails tidy. Visit a nail technician for nail care.

  10. Avoid excessive nail polish

    Limit polish use to let nails breathe, using a base coat for protection.
