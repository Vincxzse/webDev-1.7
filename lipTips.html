How to Take Care of Your Lips

Your lips are essential for talking, kissing, and eating. Dealing with dry, cracked, or irritated lips can be frustrating. Here are some tips for smoother, healthier lips.

10 Steps to Avoid Dark, Dull, and Dry Lips:

  1. Exfoliate once a week

    Exfoliating removes dead skin, leaving lips smooth. Use a lip scrub, toothbrush, or washcloth. Make a scrub with 1 tbsp honey, 1 tsp olive oil, and 1 tbsp sugar. Donâ€™t over-exfoliate to avoid irritation.

  2. Protect your lips with lip balm

    Lock in moisture and shield against UV rays with an ointment-based lip balm with SPF 15. Reapply every 2 hours, especially in cold weather.

  3. Try not to lick your lips

    Licking can dry them out more. Use lip balm instead; saliva evaporates and takes moisture with it.

  4. Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated

    Hydration benefits both inside and out. Aim for 8 glasses of water daily, avoiding dehydrating liquids.

  5. Look for lip products that moisturize and hydrate

    Choose products with ingredients like castor seed oil, ceramides, and shea butter to protect lips.

  6. Stay away from lip products with fragrances or dyes

    Harsh chemicals can irritate lips. Opt for natural, fragrance-free products.

  7. Breathe through your nose

    Mouth breathing can dry out lips. Breathe through your nose when possible, and hydrate your lips.

  8. Keep metallic items away from your lips

    Metal can irritate sensitive lips. Avoid jewelry and metallic objects near your mouth.

  9. Put a humidifier in your room

    Dry air can irritate lips. A humidifier keeps lips moisturized, especially in dry climates.

  10. Stop using tobacco products

    Tobacco increases the risk of lip cancer and contributes to oral health issues.
